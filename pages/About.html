Intro Section

Ibrahim Aouli

I am an innovative software engineer with many interests and expertise. I have worked with various tools and technologies and have a background in Game Development & Digital Analytics.
I excel in communication and programming and I also have many hobbies that I explore including gaming, watching anime, swimming and exploring nature, teaching piano and arabic, religion and foods and many more.

My background

I started with my journey at the Academy Of Information Technology (AIT) completing a Bachelor Of Interactive Media specializing in Game Development.

I learned and excelled in building games of all types in the Unity Game Engine using C#.

I also developed many useful skills from 3D modelling using 3DS MAX, MAYA and ZBrush.

I found interests in mobile games and picked up pixel art as a hobby while developing mobile games.

I developed See No Evil - A VR Horror Experience.

I moved onto building my understanding of the world of web and found myself building skills for web programming.

I started and progressed through a Web Development Bootcamp taught by Colt Steele.

I began experimenting with HTML, CSS and JavaScript and eventually got hired into a role in digital analytics.

I started working for a developing business providing analytics consulting to large enterprise businesses.

I found myself progressing forward with my skills in business analysis as I learnt Adobe tools and quickly established myself as a reliable worker and collegue.

I performed many roles from analysing and supporting the development of analytics business requirements,

writing up solution designs guiding developers with clear instructions,

validating, training and supporting developers with implementation of tags,

managing and improving on a custom tag management system and data transformation codebase,

setting up rules, dimensions and metrics using the Adobe Experience Suite,

providing quality assurance testing, documentation, data governance,

and finally building dashboard and reports based on collected data for business insights and data-driven decision making capabilities.

Following through, I continue to endeavor to build my expertise in the field of analytics and software engineering.

I take pride in my ability to innovate, automate and optimize solutions using my programming experience, communication and presentation skills.
