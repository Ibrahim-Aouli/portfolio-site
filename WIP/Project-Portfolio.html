My thought processes

The first thing I need to keep in mind when building a site is the goals and intention of the site.

I asked myself questions like:

         What is the goal of this site?

         What do we want to say?

         What do we want to show?

 

but before we can be broad, lets narrow down what is necessary.

Any portfolio site would need:

         A home page

         A contact section

         An about Me section

         Some kind of gallery or way to present my work

If I know I need these for a portfolio then let me narrow down what the goal of each of these pages is.
